Tomato Dec 10, 2024

Late Blight Destroying My Tomato Crop

RK
Ram Kumar Chitwan District

Symptoms:

I noticed dark brown spots appearing on the leaves of my tomato plants. The spots started small but quickly spread. Within a week, the leaves turned yellow and started wilting. The fruits also developed brown, firm patches that made them unmarketable.

Treatment:

I consulted with the local agriculture office and they recommended copper-based fungicide spray. I applied it every 7 days for 3 weeks. I also removed all infected leaves and destroyed them away from the field. The disease stopped spreading after the second application.

Potato Dec 8, 2024

Black Scurf on Potato Tubers

SM
Sita Maya Kavre District

Symptoms:

When I harvested my potatoes, I found black, hard masses stuck to the skin of the tubers. Some potatoes had cracked skin and the plants had shown stunted growth during the season. The stems near the soil had white fungal growth.

Treatment:

For next season, I'm treating seed potatoes with fungicide before planting. I've also started rotating crops - not planting potatoes in the same field for 3 years. Using certified disease-free seeds helped my neighbor avoid this problem.

Cabbage Dec 5, 2024

Black Rot Spreading in Cabbage Field

BP
Bishnu Prasad Dhading District

Symptoms:

The leaves of my cabbage plants developed V-shaped yellow areas starting from the leaf edges. The veins turned black and the affected areas became dry and papery. Some heads started rotting from inside with a bad smell.

Treatment:

I removed all infected plants immediately and burned them. For prevention, I now soak seeds in hot water (50°C for 25 minutes) before planting. Avoiding overhead irrigation and working in the field when plants are wet also helps prevent spread.

Chili Dec 3, 2024

Anthracnose Ruining Chili Harvest

KD
Krishna Devi Bara District

Symptoms:

My ripe chili fruits developed sunken, circular spots that turned dark. The spots had concentric rings and sometimes showed orange-pink spore masses in humid weather. Both green and red chilies were affected, causing huge losses.

Treatment:

I started spraying Mancozeb fungicide at flowering stage and continued every 10 days. Harvesting fruits before they over-ripen and removing infected fruits from the field helped reduce spread. Next year, I'll use resistant varieties.

Cucumber Nov 28, 2024

Powdery Mildew on Cucumber Leaves

GT
Ganga Thapa Rupandehi District

Symptoms:

White powdery patches appeared on the upper surface of cucumber leaves. It started on older leaves and spread to younger ones. Severely affected leaves turned yellow and dried up. The fruits remained small and misshapen.

Treatment:

I tried a home remedy first - mixing 1 tablespoon baking soda with 1 liter water and spraying weekly. It helped slow the spread. For severe cases, sulfur-based fungicide works better. Good air circulation between plants is important for prevention.

Onion Nov 25, 2024

Purple Blotch Disease in Onion

HS
Hari Sharma Banke District

Symptoms:

Small water-soaked spots appeared on onion leaves that turned purple-brown with yellow borders. The spots enlarged and joined together, causing leaves to fall over. Bulb development was severely affected and many bulbs rotted in storage.

Treatment:

I applied Mancozeb + Carbendazim combination spray every 10-15 days starting when I first saw symptoms. Proper spacing between plants and avoiding excess nitrogen fertilizer helped reduce disease severity. Storing only healthy, well-cured bulbs prevented storage losses.
