Tomato Dec 10, 2024

Late Blight Destroying My Tomato Crop

RK
Ram Kumar Chitwan District

Symptoms:

I noticed dark brown spots appearing on the leaves of my tomato plants. The spots started small but quickly spread. Within a week, the leaves turned yellow and started wilting.

Treatment:

I consulted with the local agriculture office and they recommended copper-based fungicide spray. I applied it every 7 days for 3 weeks.

Potato Dec 8, 2024

Black Scurf on Potato Tubers

SM
Sita Maya Kavre District

Symptoms:

When I harvested my potatoes, I found black, hard masses stuck to the skin of the tubers. Some potatoes had cracked skin.

Treatment:

For next season, I'm treating seed potatoes with fungicide before planting. I've also started rotating crops.

18 farmers found this helpful
Chili Dec 3, 2024

Anthracnose Ruining Chili Harvest

KD
Krishna Devi Bara District

Symptoms:

My ripe chili fruits developed sunken, circular spots that turned dark. The spots had concentric rings.

Treatment:

I started spraying Mancozeb fungicide at flowering stage and continued every 10 days.
